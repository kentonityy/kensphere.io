About Me

Hi, I'm Kenneth Palasigue and as a freelance singer, my musical journey has been a dynamic and fulfilling adventure. The freedom of being an independent artist allows me to explore various genres and styles, from soulful ballads to energetic pop tunes, adapting my voice to suit the unique character of each composition. Collaborating with a wide array of clients and musicians has broadened my musical horizons and enriched my craft. Whether I'm lending my vocals to studio recordings, live performances, or special events, I relish the opportunity to convey emotions and tell stories through song. As a freelance singer, I find immense satisfaction in bringing the joy of music to diverse audiences, making each performance a memorable and harmonious experience.

My Work

Club Singer

A club singer is a professional vocalist who specializes in performing live music in nightclubs, bars, lounges, and other entertainment venues. My primary role is to entertain and engage the audience by singing a repertoire of songs that cater to the club's atmosphere and clientele.

Band Member

Being a band member involves teamwork, collaboration, and a shared commitment to creating music. Each member contributes their unique skills and talents to help the band achieve its creative goals and deliver memorable performances to their audience.
